## Thesis (1 paragraph)

**Hold Full-Stop on the CS alpha research budget, and spend the next calendar quarter of budget on productizing honest liquid beta capital — not on PEAD tape, Form-4 F0, residual knobs, or curiosity re-runs.** Round 2’s ordered program was fully executed where it mattered: residual_hope DIE, R2-1 xs-reversal **kill_line** (gross clustered t ≈ 1.43 vs G1 ≥ 3.0; year share ≈ 44% vs 25% family law; mean-spread net at 10 bps ≤ 0; buffering fail-closed), PEAD F0 **kill_line** (D0 protocol freeze only; D1–D7 unmeasured because no original-as-published SF1/SEC tape; returns never measured). Re-opening PEAD to “finish F0 after buying SF1” or Form-4 because it was third in the queue would convert process success into sunk-cost theater. Capital allocation and alpha research are different products; the only honest next dollar is liquid market exposure without a CS claim, plus non-claim engineering that cannot create an accept path.

## Mechanism (why this path is correct economically / process-wise)

Three mechanisms, not one narrative.

**1. Research economics after joint nulls.**  
The program already paid for the cheapest falsification available on existing tape (R2-1) and the densest public-fundamental feasibility probe that could run without a new data object (PEAD F0). Both published kill_line. Opportunity cost of engineering weeks *before* a first falsifiable return number is first-class evidence (shared brief principle 13). PEAD’s failure mode was not “almost an edge”; it was **data-object absence** under a freeze that forbids analyst-SUE rescue, ranking rescue, and threshold retuning. Funding SF1/SEC original tape *now* is exactly the trap Full-Stop names: build before honest stop. Form-4 never cleared density math and was co-equal with Full-Stop as dual-exit — sequencing lost after two prior kills. Residual remains hard/narrow frozen; Gate 1a PASS was never go-live.

**2. Capital arithmetic vs alpha theater.**  
After active CS claims fail promotion gates, the rational capital default is not “paper another signal family.” It is **passive/liquid equity (and policy) exposure without pretending CS edge**. Sharpe’s arithmetic: the average actively held dollar underperforms the market by fees and costs. Post-discovery decay and multiple-testing literature make another underpowered CS line a tax on capital, not a strategy. Bessembinder’s skewness result reinforces *why* concentration gates exist: positive means can be right-tail lottery; median and year-share discipline are not pedantry. This repo already proved that lesson on residual (2020 ≈ 85.7% of after-cost net; S2 trade−SPY excess ≤ 0) and again on R2-1 (year share ≈ 44%; median spread > 0 was not enough).

**3. Process integrity is the edge we actually demonstrated.**  
Full-Stop is a **valid success mode** for research process (brief principle 10). The stack has PIT Sharadar SEP+ACTIONS+TICKERS, pure helpers, fail-closed probes, and published nulls. That is institutional-grade *falsification infrastructure*, not a license to keep inventing lines until one greens. Event-only re-open remains open as law — but re-open must pay density/feasibility first, name dual-exit (kill/Full-Stop as success), and must not re-litigate residual / R2-1 / PEAD nulls by default. Until a human PRD+grill does that, **capital is beta; engineering is non-claim**.
