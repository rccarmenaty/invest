## Mechanism (why edge should exist economically)

### Why residual packaging is dead (settled — not re-litigated)

1. **Mean residual ≠ median residual ≠ portfolio residual.** Right-tail means can clear clustered-t while the typical event underperforms peers. A 20-slot lottery over seven years (n=200) is a sparse sample of that tail.
2. **Opportunity cost is binding.** S2 is not calendar buy-hold theater: it is open-to-open SPY on each trade’s entry→exit window and notional. Mean excess ≈ −102 means capital occupied by the residual underperformed liquid equity on the strategy’s own clock.
3. **Year concentration is family law.** 2020 capturing ~86% of net profit means the structure is a sparse tail harvest, not a stable process. K2 already required leave-dominant-year economics to remain material; it did not.

### Why Form-4 is still the only *new-information* CS bet worth a multi-week tape

Corporate officers/directors already hold undiversified firm risk. A discretionary code-P Table-I acquisition adds more of that risk — a costly action, not a free opinion. Two distinct opportunistic buyers (prior-history routine/opportunistic split from complete prior years only) reduces single-person liquidity noise. The economic claim is **slow incorporation of private information after public filing availability**, not transaction-date insider alpha and not “insiders always win.”

Sharp mechanism predictions (fail these → kill the *story*, not just the book):

1. Opportunistic two-buyer clusters beat routine two-buyer clusters (same liquidity/timing).  
2. Two distinct buyers beat single opportunistic buyers.  
3. Filing-time / next-open abnormal return survives costs; transaction-date-only edge is **not** the claim.  
4. Liquid subset still shows positive economics (not microcap mirage).  
5. Edge is not pure value/reversal disguise after characteristic matching.

This is a **different economic object** from the frozen residual (price path → costly informed actions). It is also different from short-horizon CS reversal (liquidity provision inventory risk) and from PEAD (public accounting innovation). Each can be true or false independently; only Form-4 changes the information source to *actions of informed agents*.

## Concrete strategy design (entries, ranking, exits, portfolio, costs assumptions)

### Dual-track program: **FOFS — Form-4 or Full-Stop**

**Track 0 — Capital (not research).** Hold honest liquid equity exposure (SPY/IWM or equivalent low-cost vehicle). No CS claim. Optional *transparent* multi-asset TS sleeve is out of scope for this repo’s US daily equity stack unless a separate PRD names futures data; do **not** backtest single-name TSMOM on 2019–2025 as “honest beta.”

**Track 1 — Research (only competing CS line).**

**Public-time data contract.** Immutable SEC ownership filings: `valid_time` = transaction date; `known_time` = EDGAR acceptance / first public availability. Form 4/A is a prospective partial correction, never a rewrite of the original signal row. Map CIK + security title + event-date symbol to one period-valid listed share class; quarantine ambiguity. Code-P Table-I acquisitions are honestly labeled until footnotes prove open-market venue.

**Eligibility (prior session):** primary US common; price ≥ $5; 20-session median dollar volume ≥ $5M (stress higher floors).

**Qualifying buyer:** officer or director; non-derivative Table-I; `TRANS_CODE=P` and acquired; exclude pure 10% owners in primary, derivatives, awards, exercises, tax dispositions. Direct ownership primary.

**Opportunistic classifier (each Jan 1):** three previous complete calendar years only. Routine = same calendar month in all three years; adequate-history non-routine = opportunistic; else excluded.

**Cluster:** ≥2 distinct opportunistic people at same issuer become public within 10 trading sessions. Multiple rows/joint reports/amendments do not mint buyers. Collapse repeats during active hold.

**Entry:** next regular-session **open** after `known_time` completes the cluster. Halt/missing open → deterministic no-fill.

**Ranking (portfolio only):** when slots bind — buyer count ↓, disclosed purchase $ / ADV ↓, ADV ↓, ticker. **No** momentum, 52w, ID, Core composite, or trained score in primary.

**Exit:** open after **60** sessions. No price stop/TP/trail in primary. Corporate actions: actual economic consideration; policy FC never counts as alpha.

**Portfolio:** long-only; max 20 names; inverse-20d-vol sizing; 5% issuer cap; 100% gross; notional ≤ 1% prior ADV. Equal-event for diagnosis; capacity-weighted for deployability.

**Costs:** primary pre-tax after **5 bps/side** including next-open gap; stress 10 and 25 bps. Reject zero-cost-only or filing-close-entry claims.

**Track 2 — Full-Stop.** If Track 1 dies at data audit or signal/economic gates: publish the negative; **do not** open residual autopsy or DAMB packaging; research budget ends for CS alpha.

### Pre-audit density math (mandatory before tape build)

Publish **expected-n**, expected distinct entry dates, and a conservative **year-share stress** under Phase-2-like slot caps *before* multi-week plumbing. If expected-n is Phase-2-scale sparse without a credible path to year-share ≤~25%, **do not build the tape** — Full-Stop or demote to a different line.
