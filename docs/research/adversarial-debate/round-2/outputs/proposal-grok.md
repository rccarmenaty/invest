## Thesis (1 paragraph)

Joint evidence from Steps 0–7 is decisive and must not be re-litigated: the accepted price-event stream has a **mean** h60 excess-vs-universe residual that is **not pure beta** (Gate 1a: +1.89%, clustered t=5.30), but that residual is **right-tail driven** (median excess −0.42%), **regime-brittle** (2021 −6.74% vs 2024 +5.16%), **unrankable** (Gate 1b not claimed; Core already lost to §2.5), and **economically dead as a portfolio product** under the only structure that was allowed to try (Phase 2 NO-GO: 2020 = 85.7% of net profit; Phase 2b DIE: leave-2020 mean ≈9.44 ≤ half full-book mean; trade-window matched SPY excess ≈ **−102** after costs). That joint pattern is not “tune the residual”; it is the signature that **event-level mean residual ≠ harvestable CS alpha under capital constraints vs liquid beta**. The strongest next research program is therefore a **binary, budget-capped pivot**: (A) open a **new named line** — opportunistic multi-insider Form-4 *purchase* clusters timed from public EDGAR availability, next-open entry, fixed 60-session hold, no price stop — **only after** a PIT data audit, with **Phase-2 failure modes pre-registered as kill gates** (year concentration, trade-window SPY economic residual bar, median-vs-mean honesty, mechanism orderings, no residual autopsy); or (B) if the audit fails or the filing-time event study fails those gates, **full-stop CS-alpha research** on this repo’s research budget and reallocate capital-research time to non-claim engineering + honest liquid exposure productization. Anything that reopens ranking, Quiet Drift packaging, DAMB modules, “drop 2020,” or a second residual autopsy is **out of bounds** under the freeze and is research malpractice given S2.

## Mechanism (why edge should exist economically)

Corporate officers and directors already hold large, undiversified firm exposure. A **discretionary open-market (or code-P Table-I) cash acquisition** adds more of that risk and is therefore a costly action, not a free opinion. Requiring **two distinct people** within a short public window reduces the chance that the observation is one person’s liquidity or tax choice. Classifying buyers as **opportunistic vs routine from prior complete years only** (Cohen–Malloy–Pomorski style) removes calendar-pattern trading that is not information. The economic claim is **slow incorporation of private information about firm prospects after the *public* filing**, not that “insiders always win” or that transaction-date alpha is available to outsiders.

This is a different economic object from the frozen residual:

| Object | Price-event residual (dead as product) | Form-4 cluster (this proposal) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Information source | Own price/volume path | Costly actions by informed agents |
| Why residual might exist | Underreaction / common continuation | Private information + attention/limits to arbitrage |
| Why portfolio failed | Right-tail, 2020 lottery, loses to SPY windows | *Unknown* — must re-prove at filing time |
| Ranking | Failed on this cohort | Forbidden in primary spec; mechanism counts only |

Sharp, predeclared mechanism predictions (if these fail, kill the *story*, not just the book):

1. Opportunistic two-buyer clusters beat routine two-buyer clusters (same liquidity/timing).  
2. Two distinct buyers beat single opportunistic buyers.  
3. Filing-time / next-open abnormal return survives costs; transaction-date-only edge is **not** the claim.  
4. Liquid subset (not microcaps) still shows positive economics.  
5. Edge is not pure value/reversal disguise after characteristic matching.

If (1)–(2) fail while a raw long-only book is green, you have beta or small-cap lottery again — Phase 2 already taught that lesson.

## Concrete strategy design (entries, ranking, exits, portfolio, costs assumptions)

### Dual-path program name
**FOFS — Form-4 or Full-Stop**

Path A is the only *strategy* design. Path B is an explicit research outcome, not a failure to decide.

### Path A — primary event (frozen before returns)

**Public-time data contract.** Immutable SEC ownership filings with bitemporal fields: `valid_time` = transaction date; `known_time` = EDGAR acceptance / first public availability. Form 4/A is a **prospective partial correction**, never a rewrite of the original signal row. Map CIK + security title + event-date symbol to one period-valid listed share class; quarantine ambiguity. Code-P Table-I acquisitions are honestly labeled “Form-4 code-P Table-I acquisition” until footnotes/vendor field prove open-market venue (SEC Form 4 instructions / ownership codes).

**Eligibility (prior session):** primary US common; price ≥ $5; 20-session median dollar volume ≥ $5M (predeclared; liquid subset stress higher).

**Qualifying buyer:** officer or director; non-derivative Table-I; `TRANS_CODE=P` and acquired; exclude pure 10% owners in primary, derivatives, awards, exercises, tax dispositions, invalid prices/shares. Direct ownership primary; indirect = preregistered sensitivity only.

**Opportunistic classifier (each Jan 1):** using only the **three previous complete calendar years**. Routine = at least one trade in the same calendar month in all three years; adequate-history non-routine = opportunistic; else unclassified (excluded). No look-ahead into the test year.

**Cluster completion:** filings from **≥2 distinct opportunistic people** at the same issuer become public within **10 trading sessions**. Multiple rows / joint reports / amendments do **not** mint extra buyers. Collapse repeat clusters during an active hold.

**Entry:** next regular-session **open** after the `known_time` that completes the cluster. No same-day close fantasy. Halt/missing open → deterministic no-fill; never prior close.

**Ranking (portfolio only; not signal claim):** when slots bind — (1) distinct qualifying-buyer count ↓, (2) aggregate disclosed purchase $ / prior-day median ADV ↓, (3) prior-day ADV ↓, (4) ticker deterministic. **No** momentum, 52w, ID, Core composite, or trained score in the primary specification.

**Exit:** open after **60** completed sessions. No price stop, TP, or trail in primary (Phase 2 structure lesson on short-path noise still applies to *any* slow event). h20/h120 are diagnostics in the declared family only. Corporate actions receive actual economic consideration; policy FC never counts as alpha (Step 1).

**Portfolio:** long-only; max **20** names; inverse-20d-vol sizing; 5% equity cap per issuer; 100% gross; order notional ≤ 1% prior-day median ADV. Equal-event for diagnosis; capacity-weighted for deployability.

**Costs:** primary = pre-tax after **5 bps/side** including realized next-open gap; tax 15% on gains secondary. Stress 10 and 25 bps. Reject zero-cost-only or filing-close-entry-only claims.

### Path B — full-stop (predeclared success condition for *honesty*)

If Path A dies at data audit or signal/economic gates: publish the negative; **do not** open residual autopsy, ranking rescue, or DAMB packaging; research budget shifts to non-claim engineering and/or an **explicitly non-alpha** liquid exposure product (see Attacks C). Path B is a valid terminal state of this program.

## Acceptance / rejection gates

### Data (F1) — accept only if
- ≥95% of sampled qualifying events reconcile to immutable source + unique period-valid security.  
- Zero detected lookahead / retrospective amendment rewrites in audit sample.  
- Every inactive held name has explicit terminal economics path.  
- Estimated primary-filter n is large enough for annual folds (predeclare minimum event count in F0; if below floor → Path B, not rule relaxation).

**Else → Path B.**

### Signal (F2) — accept only if **all** hold
1. Preregistered **h60** next-open after 5 bps **abnormal** (excess vs same-date eligible universe primary; factor residual if available) > 0 with family-wise-adjusted evidence excluding zero.  
2. Positive in a **majority** of annual walk-forward folds.  
3. **Median** h60 excess ≥ 0 **or** predeclared weaker alternative: hit rate > 50% with CI above 50% — *must* confront right-tail-only failure mode from Gate 1a. (If only mean is positive and median deeply negative, **reject** as Phase-2-style lottery.)  
4. Opportunistic-cluster − routine-cluster spread > 0 in predicted direction.  
5. Two-buyer ≥ single-buyer in predicted direction.  
6. Liquid subset (stricter ADV floor, predeclared) still positive mean abnormal at h60 after costs.

**Else → Path B (program kill of Form-4 line). No residual autopsy.**

### Portfolio / promotion (F3) — accept only if **all** hold
1. After-cost exp > 0 on majority of WF folds (full and FC-segregated).  
2. **No calendar entry-year > ~25% of after-cost net profit.**  
3. **S2-style economic residual bar:** mean (trade after-cost − matched SPY window P&L) > 0 on full book **and** on leave-dominant-year book.  
4. Capacity-weighted / ADV-capped book still clears (1)–(3) at 5 bps; stress at 10 bps does not flip sign of mean exp on majority folds.  
5. Mechanism-rank admission does **not** *require* beating random admission for signal diagnosis — but if rank is used in the promoted book, it must not be the sole source of green (report both).

**Else → Path B.** Do **not** invent K2-style “residual hope” on Form-4. DIE means stop.

### Standing rejects
- Edge only at transaction date / filing close.  
- Edge only in microcaps or before costs.  
- Edge only at h120 or only one horizon cherry-pick.  
- Edge only with omitted delistings / current tickers / cleaned amendments.  
- Green only after post-hoc classifier relaxation.  
- Any accept path that depends on FC policy P&L.  
- Any re-entry into frozen Phase 2 residual claim.

## Attacks on the other two archetypes

### A) Form-4 opportunistic clusters — Round 1 Codex Phase 3 *as written*

Codex’s protocol hygiene remains the best of Round 1: bitemporal known_time, code-P honesty, delisting discipline, capacity-weighted reject, mechanism orderings, next-open only. **That design quality is not the issue.**

What is wrong *now*, after Steps 5–7:

1. **It underweights portfolio economics.** Phase 2 proved you can have majority positive folds and still be un-promotable (2020 concentration) and economically worse than SPY on matched trade windows (S2 −102). Round 1 Form-4 gates mention year share and factor alpha, but they do not elevate **trade-window matched liquid beta** and **leave-dominant-year economic residual** to the same status as the signal t-stat. Without that, Form-4 can green an event study and still ship Phase-2’s failure mode in a suit.

2. **It underweights right-tail / median failure.** Gate 1a median excess was negative while mean cleared. Codex’s signal accept is mean-abnormal + folds + spreads. A Form-4 “mean cluster premium” with lottery medians is the same statistical shape we already refuse to promote.

3. **Opportunity cost without a hard stop branch.** “Build the tape” is unbounded. Round 2 must force **F1 budget cap + Path B**. Otherwise Form-4 is an engineering identity project.

4. **Automatic Phase 3 sequencing is obsolete.** Meta-judge said Form-4 after Phase 2 *settles*. It settled **negative on the residual**. Form-4 is no longer a free continuation reward; it is a **competing new line** that must earn budget against full-stop.

**Refine Codex; do not worship it. Do not skip F1. Do not promote on mean alone.**

### B) Rescue / rebrand residual price events — ranking, gates, vol-scale, DAMB modules, “just drop 2020,” second autopsy

This is the **most dangerous archetype right now**.

Why:

- The freeze **explicitly** forbids structure tweaks, second residual autopsy, ranking, Quiet Drift/DAMB packaging as continuation, and rewriting the ~25% year-share gate.  
- Gate 1a PASS is catnip for narrative: “residual exists, so fix the book.” Phase 2b already measured the only residual hope that was allowed — **DIE**.  
- Adding ID ranking (Quiet Drift) after Gate 1b failed is Step 3 amnesia.  
- Adding SPY 200DMA / vol-scale / trail grids (DAMB leftover) after a structure test that already removed stops and used fixed horizon is **confounding a dead product with market timing** — the classic way to mint green beta and call it research.  
- “Drop 2020” or soften year-share is **p-hacking the promotion rule**.  
- A second autopsy moves goalposts after K2/S2 were predeclared.

**Any proposal that spends the next dollar on the frozen residual claim is research malpractice under the published freeze, regardless of prose quality.**

### C) Honest beta / passive — stop claiming CS alpha; productize SPY/IWM or simple TSMOM with costs

This is the **second-most dangerous archetype if mis-sequenced**, and the **correct terminal state if Form-4 dies**.

Danger now:

1. **Premature surrender of the only remaining high-information CS bet.** Gate 1a showed non-zero mean residual *exists* on some event definition; that does not resurrect the residual *portfolio*, but it also does not prove all CS information sources are dead. Killing Form-4 *before* a cheap F1 audit is emotional overfit to Phase 2 pain.

2. **False confidence in “we productize beta.”** Building a polished SPY/IWM wrapper or futures-style TSMOM on equities can absorb the entire engineering budget while pretending to be strategy research. TSMOM literature is futures-heavy (Moskowitz–Ooi–Pedersen); equity single-name after costs is unpaid debt — same debt DAMB never paid.

3. **Hiding selection failure as risk management.** “Just hold SPY” is honest *capital* advice; it is not a research program unless framed as **Path B of FOFS** after F1/F2 fail.

When C is right: after F1 fails or F2/F3 fail — **then** full-stop CS alpha and allocate production capital to liquid beta/TS products without cosplay.
